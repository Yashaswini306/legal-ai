About

Built for the people, not law firms

LegalShield AI democratizes legal understanding. Every person deserves to know what they're signing.

🎯 Our Mission

Legal documents are intentionally complex. We use AI to level the playing field — giving everyone the same understanding of their rights that was previously only available to those who could afford lawyers.

🔒 Privacy First

Your documents are never stored or shared. All analysis happens in real time and is discarded immediately. We don't log, track, or sell your data.

LegalShield AI provides informational analysis only. It is not a substitute for professional legal advice. Always consult a qualified lawyer for important legal matters.
